User Agreement


RAYVID is committed to patient safety and privacy. The RAVID website is a communication tool for medical professionals, but should not be used by itself to make clinical decisions. It is the user's responsibility to ensure that no patient protected information is uploaded to the RAYVID website. Uploaded content is either deleted by the user after viewing or automatically by the application 24 hours after it is uploaded. It is the user's responsibility to manage links provided by the RAYVID website accordingly.

About


RAYVID is a communication tool for medical professionals. Its primary intended use is for radiologists to share anonymized screen capture videos of radiology findings with other physcians and patients. Beyond this, the service can be used to share other anonymized medical-related communications. To help protect patient privacy, all uploaded content is deleted within 24 hours or directly by the user.
